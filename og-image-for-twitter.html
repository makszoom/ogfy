Twitter OG Image FAQ

What is an OG image for Twitter?

An OG image (Open Graph image) is the preview image that appears when you share a Twitter link on social media platforms like Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, and others. A well-designed OG image can significantly increase your click-through rate.

What size should a Twitter OG image be?

The optimal size for Twitter OG images is 1200×630 pixels. This is the standard Open Graph image size that displays correctly across all major social media platforms including Twitter, Facebook, and LinkedIn.
